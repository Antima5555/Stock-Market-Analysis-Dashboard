# 🚀Stock Market Analysis Dashboard

A data-driven Power BI dashboard to analyze stock market performance, risk, and trends for better investment decision-making.

---

## 📌 Business Problem

In financial markets, investors and portfolio managers need to decide where to invest to maximize returns while minimizing risk. However, stock prices are highly volatile and influenced by multiple factors such as market conditions, economic events, and company performance.

Without proper data analysis, investment decisions can lead to significant financial losses.

---

## 🎯 Objective

This project aims to analyze historical stock data of NIFTY-50 companies to:

* Identify top-performing and underperforming stocks
* Measure risk using volatility and drawdown
* Analyze market trends over time
* Understand industry-wise contribution
* Enable data-driven investment decisions

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* **Python** – Data cleaning, feature engineering, EDA
* **MySQL** – Data storage and querying
* **Power BI** – Dashboard creation and visualization

---

## 📊 Dashboard Overview

### 🔹 1. Market Overview

* Total market volume
* Average daily returns
* Stock price trends over time
* Industry-wise contribution

### 🔹 2. Risk & Volatility Analysis

* Volatility trends
* Risk-return distribution
* Top volatile stocks
* Worst drawdown analysis

### 🔹 3. Performance Analysis

* Top performing stocks
* Worst performing stocks
* Industry-wise performance
* Return trends over time

---

## 💡 Key Insights

* Higher volatility stocks indicate higher risk and unstable returns
* Certain sectors (like Financial Services) dominate market activity
* There is a significant gap between best and worst-performing stocks
* Risk-return tradeoff is clearly visible across stocks
